# 📊 Customer Behavior Analysis

A data analytics project focused on analyzing customer shopping behavior using **Python, SQL, and Power BI** to extract insights and support data-driven business decisions.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes customer shopping behavior from transactional data (~3,900 records) to understand:
- Customer purchasing patterns  
- Product preferences  
- Subscription impact  
- Revenue trends  

The goal is to help businesses improve **customer engagement, marketing strategies, and overall sales performance**.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

A retail company wants to understand customer behavior across:
- Demographics  
- Product categories  
- Shopping patterns  

### Key Questions:
- What factors influence customer purchases?
- Which customer segments generate more revenue?
- How do discounts and subscriptions affect buying behavior?

---

## 📂 Dataset Information

- 📊 Total Records: **3,900**
- 📌 Columns: **18**
- 🔍 Features include:
  - Customer demographics (Age, Gender, Location)
  - Purchase details (Item, Category, Amount, Season)
  - Behavioral data (Discounts, Reviews, Frequency)

- ⚠️ Missing Values:
  - Review Rating column (handled during preprocessing)

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- 🐍 Python (Pandas, NumPy)
- 🗄️ PostgreSQL (SQL Queries)
- 📊 Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)

---

## 🔄 Project Workflow

### 1️⃣ Data Preparation (Python)
- Data cleaning and preprocessing  
- Handling missing values  
- Feature engineering (age_group, purchase_frequency)  
- Data transformation  

---

### 2️⃣ Data Analysis (SQL)
- Revenue analysis by gender  
- High-spending customers identification  
- Top-rated products  
- Customer segmentation (New, Returning, Loyal)  
- Subscription impact analysis  

---

### 3️⃣ Data Visualization (Power BI)
Created an interactive dashboard including:
- KPI Cards (Total Customers, Avg Purchase, Avg Rating)
- Revenue by Category
- Sales by Category
- Customer Distribution (Subscription Status)
- Revenue & Sales by Age Group

---

## 📊 Key Insights

- Majority of customers are **non-subscribers**
- **Young Adults** contribute highest revenue
- Discounts influence purchasing behavior significantly
- Certain products have higher dependency on discounts
- Loyal customers form the largest segment

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Increase subscription benefits to boost retention  
- Implement loyalty programs for repeat customers  
- Optimize discount strategies to balance profit & sales  
- Focus marketing on high-revenue customer segments  
- Promote top-rated and best-selling products
